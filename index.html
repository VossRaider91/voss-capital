Bed & ISA Optimisation Plan
Maximises ISA transfer within CGT limit
Sells GIA holdings and moves proceeds to ISA. Strictly stays within your £3,000 CGT allowance. Holdings at a loss or zero gain are transferred first — free of CGT. Then lowest-gain holdings are used until the CGT limit is reached. If the full £20,000 ISA allowance cannot be used, it means your remaining holdings would all generate gains above the available allowance.
Records the sale in Monthly History, removes units from your GIA, and updates CGT used this year
